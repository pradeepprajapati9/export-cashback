e-BRC pending / not showing on DGFT — how to fix

Foreign payment has arrived in your bank, but the e-BRC (Electronic Bank Realization Certificate) isn't showing on DGFT — so your cashback stays on hold. Here's how to get it updated.

Why e-BRC gets stuck

  • Your bank hasn't uploaded the Inward Remittance Message (IRM) to the DGFT/IDPMS system.
  • The remittance isn't matched to the correct shipping bill / invoice in IDPMS.
  • Purpose code or amount mismatch between the payment and the export.

Step-by-step fix

  1. Confirm the remittance — Check with your AD (Authorized Dealer) bank that the foreign payment is received and recorded.
  2. Ask the bank to file the IRM — Request the bank to upload the Inward Remittance Message and match it to your export in IDPMS.
  3. Self-certify the e-BRC on DGFT — Once the IRM is available, log in to the DGFT portal and generate/self-certify the e-BRC against your shipping bill.
  4. Check purpose code & amount — Ensure the purpose code and realized amount match the export invoice.
  5. Track status — Verify the e-BRC now shows on DGFT so the linked incentive can be released.
Time matters: incentives tied to realization have deadlines. A pending e-BRC can silently block RoDTEP and other benefits — chase your bank early.
