RoDTEP scroll / scrip not generating — how to fix

Your RoDTEP amount shows in the ledger on ICEGATE, but the e-scrip won't generate and you can't use or sell it? Here's what's blocking it and how to fix it.

Why the scrip won't generate

CauseWhat it means
Scrip not created manuallyThe amount sits in the RoDTEP ledger — you still have to generate the e-scrip from it.
IEC not registered on ICEGATEYour IEC + DSC must be registered on ICEGATE to create a scrip.
Scroll not yet generatedCustoms generates the scroll per shipping bill first; if pending, the ledger won't fill.
Bank / AD code issueAD code or bank account not registered at the port of export.

Step-by-step fix

  1. Register on ICEGATE — Ensure your IEC is registered with a valid DSC on the ICEGATE portal.
  2. Check the scroll — Confirm Customs has generated the RoDTEP scroll for your shipping bills (the ledger should show a credit).
  3. Generate the e-scrip — Go to ICEGATE → Financial Services → RoDTEP/RoSCTL, select the ledger credits and generate the e-scrip.
  4. Verify AD code / bank — Make sure your AD code and bank account are registered at the export port.
  5. Use or transfer — Once generated, the scrip can be used to pay customs duty or transferred/sold.
Don't delay: RoDTEP ledger credits have a validity window to convert into scrips. Generate them in time or you may lose the benefit.
