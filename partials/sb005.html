SB005 Error Fix: Unblock your stuck IGST refund

Seeing an SB005 error and your IGST refund hasn't hit your bank? The money isn't lost — it's blocked by a data mismatch. Here's why it happens and the step-by-step fix.

What is the SB005 error?

SB005 is the most common error that blocks IGST refunds on exports. It means the invoice details you filed in your GST return (GSTR-1) do not match the invoice details on your Shipping Bill (Customs / ICEGATE). Because the two government systems can't confirm it's the same invoice, the system holds your refund instead of releasing it.

Why does it happen? (Common causes)

Mismatch inTypical mistake
Invoice NumberDifferent number in GSTR-1 vs Shipping Bill (extra space, prefix, /)
Invoice ValueTaxable / invoice value differs between the two
Invoice DateDate entered differently
GSTIN / PortWrong GSTIN or port code

Step-by-step fix

  1. Confirm it's SB005 — On ICEGATE, check the refund/scroll status of the shipping bill; the error code shown will be SB005.
  2. Find the mismatch — Put the GSTR-1 invoice line and the Shipping Bill invoice line side by side; compare number, value and date. Usually it's one small difference.
  3. Choose a correction route — (a) Use the ICEGATE online correction utility to update the mismatched detail, or (b) Officer Interface — a Customs officer manually verifies and sanctions the refund (CBIC provides this as a permanent facility for SB005).
  4. Fix the GST side if needed — If the mistake was in your GST return, amend the invoice in GSTR-1 Table 9A so it matches the Shipping Bill.
  5. Re-validate & track — After the correction the system reprocesses and the scroll regenerates. Keep checking ICEGATE until the amount is sanctioned.
Time-limit warning: Don't sit on it. Expired claim windows and late corrections are exactly how exporters permanently lose their refund. Fix an SB005 as soon as you spot it.
