PFMS / bank account validation error — how to fix

Your refund is sanctioned but not credited because the bank account failed PFMS validation? This is a common, fixable block. Here's how to clear it.

Why PFMS validation fails

  • Bank account number or IFSC on ICEGATE doesn't match bank records.
  • Account name doesn't match the IEC / GSTIN holder's name.
  • Account is dormant, closed, or not linked correctly in the PFMS system.

Step-by-step fix

  1. Check the error — On ICEGATE, view the refund status; a PFMS/account validation failure will be flagged.
  2. Verify your bank details — Confirm the exact account number, IFSC and account holder name with your bank.
  3. Update the account on ICEGATE — Add/correct the bank account under the bank-account management section and submit for validation.
  4. Re-trigger validation — The account goes for PFMS re-validation; once it passes, the refund is credited.
  5. Confirm credit — Track until the sanctioned amount hits your account.
Tip: use an active current account in the exact business name to avoid repeat validation failures.
